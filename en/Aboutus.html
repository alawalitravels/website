About Al Awali Travels

Al Awali Travels has established itself as one of Kuwait’s leading travel agencies, trusted by generations of travelers for over 40 years. We provide a full range of travel services, including flight and hotel bookings, travel insurance, car rentals, tailor-made holiday packages, rail journeys, and cruises worldwide.

Our team is dedicated to delivering reliable, personalized, and professional service, ensuring that every trip is carefully planned and executed to meet the unique needs of each client. Whether it’s a business trip, a family vacation, or a luxury holiday, we aim to make every journey seamless and memorable.

Our Mission: To offer exceptional travel experiences at competitive prices, combining expertise, dedication, and attention to detail so that you can explore the world with confidence and ease.

We warmly invite you to visit any of our Al Awali office to discuss and plan your future travels in person.
